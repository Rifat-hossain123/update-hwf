Our Services

  • 1. Interest free loan to active poor, lower/lower-middle class people.
  • 2. Financial support to poor & helpless people, poor & talented students, poor orphans.
  • 3. Financial support during marriage of poor girls.
  • 4. Support during any natural disasters.
  • 5. Collection of "ZAKAT" & it's distribution to determined sectors.
  • 6. Support to disabled person.
  • 7. Any public & social services.

Terms and Conditions

  • 1. It's a Nonprofit Organization.
  • 2. Your donation is non-refundable.
  • 3. You can donate as a permanent donor or guest donor.
  • 4. As a permanent donor you must have to donate a monthly minimum amount determined by the organization.
  • 5. Anytime you can resign or rejoin as a permanent donor.
  • 6. Permanent donor can donate any amount above the determined minimum amount.
  • 7. Guest donor anytime can donate any amount.
